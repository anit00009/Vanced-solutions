Engineering FAQ

Everything you need to know about our product engineering methodology.

We specialize in modern stacks like React/Next.js for front-end, Node.js/Python for back-end, and AWS/Azure for infrastructure. However, we always choose the tool that best fits the project.
We have deep experience in refactoring and modernizing legacy systems. We typically use a "Strangler Fig" pattern to gradually replace old components without disrupting service.
